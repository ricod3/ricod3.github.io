My motivation for
Information Technology

I have always been interested in Information Technology, which is why I chose Cloud Computing as the topic for my Matura project. At that time, I was aware that the IT industry will experience an almost exponential growth.

My interest in IT was further strengthened by a training program I completed at KPMG. I have witnessed and experienced the IT industry to a very large extent, because we had contact with various companies/large enterprises and therefore know what the current challenges in the IT sector are.
I learned many things from messing around with Linux to building a small Cloud Setup in Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud Platform, where we were able to deploy our applications. While I was doing these tasks which, of course, took a lot of time for troubleshooting, I always thought how interesting and wide-ranging the IT industry really is.

I always wanted to be a part of the IT sector, I always wanted to contribute something valuable to the world or at least to Austria and I think the best way to achieve this is to learn with and about Information Technology. This is what I want to do, this is where I belong.
